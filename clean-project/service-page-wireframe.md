## Key Elements & Functionality

### Service Hero
- Clear service name as H1 heading
- Brief, compelling service description
- Strong call-to-action buttons
- Background image relevant to the service

### Service Overview
- Comprehensive description of the service
- Key features and capabilities
- High-quality image showing service in action
- Professional, authoritative tone

### Service Benefits
- 4-6 key benefits with icons
- Clear, concise benefit statements
- Customer-focused language
- Visual hierarchy to emphasize most important benefits

### Our Process
- Step-by-step visualization of service process
- Clear numbering/progression
- Brief descriptions of each step
- Icons representing each step

### Certifications
- Display of relevant industry certifications
- Brief explanation of qualifications
- Emphasis on compliance and safety
- Trust-building elements

### Pricing Guidance
- Transparent information about pricing factors
- Package options if applicable
- Clear call-to-action for quotes
- No specific prices if variable by project

### Related Projects
- Showcase of 2-3 relevant completed projects
- High-quality project images
- Brief project descriptions
- Link to full portfolio

### Service-Specific Testimonials
- Customer testimonials specifically about this service
- Customer details for credibility
- Carousel/slider format if multiple testimonials
- Focus on results and customer satisfaction

### FAQ Section
- 3-5 most common questions about the service
- Clear, helpful answers
- Expandable/collapsible format
- Option to view more FAQs

### Related Services
- Links to complementary services
- Brief service descriptions
- Clear navigation to other service pages
- Cross-selling opportunity

### CTA Section
- Strong final call-to-action
- Emphasis on next steps
- Multiple contact options
- Urgency-creating language
