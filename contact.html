Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

For standard service requests, we typically respond within 24-48 hours. For emergency situations, we offer 24/7 service and aim to have a technician on-site as quickly as possible, often within 1-2 hours in the Christchurch area.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation quotes for all electrical and heat pump projects. Simply contact us through this form or give us a call, and we'll arrange for one of our technicians to assess your needs and provide a detailed quote.

What areas do you service?

We service all of Christchurch and surrounding areas, including Riccarton, Merivale, Fendalton, Cashmere, Addington, Papanui, Hornby, Sydenham, Ilam, Avonhead, and the Christchurch CBD. For locations outside these areas, please contact us to discuss availability.

Are your electricians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. All our electricians are fully licensed, certified, and insured. We maintain all necessary qualifications and stay up-to-date with the latest industry standards and safety regulations to ensure the highest quality of work.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive workmanship warranty. Additionally, all products we install come with manufacturer warranties. We'll explain the specific warranty details for your project during the quoting process.
