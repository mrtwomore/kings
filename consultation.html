Frequently Asked Questions

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, absolutely! Our consultation service is completely free with no obligation to hire us afterward. We believe in providing value upfront and helping you make informed decisions about your electrical needs.

How long does a consultation typically take?

Most consultations take between 30-60 minutes, depending on the complexity of your project. For larger projects, we may recommend a site visit which could take a bit longer to ensure we understand all aspects of your needs.

What should I prepare before the consultation?

It's helpful to have a general idea of your project goals, budget range, and timeline. If you have any existing electrical plans, recent power bills (for efficiency consultations), or photos of the areas involved, these can be very useful during our discussion.

What happens after the consultation?

After your consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed proposal including recommended solutions, pricing options, and a project timeline. You're free to take your time reviewing this information with no pressure to proceed. If you decide to move forward, we'll schedule your project at a time that works for you.

Can I get a consultation for a small project?

Absolutely! We provide consultations for projects of all sizes, from simple installations to complete electrical system overhauls. No project is too small for our advisors to help with.
