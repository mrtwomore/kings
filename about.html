About Kings Electrical

Your trusted electrical and heat pump specialists in Christchurch

Our Story

Kings Electrical has been serving the Christchurch community for over 20 years, providing professional electrical and heat pump services to residential and commercial clients.

Who We Are

Founded in 2003, Kings Electrical started as a small family business with a commitment to quality workmanship and exceptional customer service. Over the years, we've grown into one of Christchurch's most trusted electrical service providers, while maintaining our dedication to personalized service.

Our team consists of fully certified electricians and heat pump specialists with a wealth of experience across all aspects of electrical work. We pride ourselves on staying ahead of industry developments and investing in ongoing training to ensure we deliver the most efficient and innovative solutions to our clients.

From basic repairs to complex installations, we approach every project with the same level of care and professionalism, ensuring that our customers receive the highest quality service every time.

Our Approach

At Kings Electrical, we believe in doing things right the first time. Our approach is built on three key principles:

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our workmanship or the materials we use.
  • Transparency: We provide clear, detailed quotes with no hidden costs or surprises.
  • Reliability: We show up when we say we will and complete projects within the agreed timeframe.

We understand that inviting service providers into your home or business requires trust. That's why we go the extra mile to ensure every interaction with Kings Electrical exceeds your expectations.

Our Core Values

These fundamental principles guide everything we do at Kings Electrical.

Excellence

Our Journey

The evolution of Kings Electrical from a small local business to a trusted industry leader.

2003

Humble Beginnings

Kings Electrical was founded as a one-man operation, focusing on residential electrical services in Christchurch.

2008

Expanding Services

Added heat pump installation and servicing to our offerings, expanding our team to five technicians.

2011

Post-Earthquake Rebuild

Played a significant role in Christchurch's rebuild efforts after the earthquakes, supporting our community.

2015

Commercial Division

Launched our specialized commercial services division to meet growing demand from business clients.

2020

Energy Efficiency Focus

Developed our energy efficiency assessment and solution services to help clients reduce energy costs.

2023

Today

Now a team of 15 specialists, we continue to grow while maintaining our commitment to quality and personal service.
