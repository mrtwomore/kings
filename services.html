Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

For standard service requests, we typically schedule appointments within 24-48 hours. For emergency electrical issues, we offer 24/7 service with an average response time of under 60 minutes in the Christchurch area. Our team prioritizes urgent situations to ensure your safety and minimize disruption.

What brands of heat pumps do you install?

We are authorized dealers for several premium heat pump brands including Mitsubishi Electric, Daikin, Fujitsu, and Panasonic. We select from these trusted manufacturers because of their reliability, energy efficiency, and excellent warranty support. During your consultation, we'll recommend the best system for your specific needs and space.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Absolutely. All our electrical work comes with a comprehensive 12-month workmanship warranty. For heat pump installations, we provide an extended 5-year workmanship guarantee. Additionally, all products and systems we install carry their respective manufacturer warranties, which we help you register. We're committed to your long-term satisfaction.

What areas of Christchurch do you service?

We provide service throughout greater Christchurch and surrounding areas including Riccarton, Merivale, Fendalton, Cashmere, Addington, Papanui, Hornby, Sydenham, Ilam, Avonhead, and the Christchurch CBD. For locations further out, please contact us to discuss availability and scheduling options.

Do you offer financing options for larger projects?

Yes, we understand that larger electrical upgrades and heat pump systems represent a significant investment. That's why we've partnered with trusted financing providers to offer flexible payment options with competitive rates. During your consultation, we can discuss the available financing plans to find one that fits your budget and needs.
